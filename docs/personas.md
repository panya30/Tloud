## Persona 1: แม่ค้าออนไลน์ — "Nong Ploy" (Online Seller)

### Profile

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Name** | Ploy (พลอย) |
| **Age** | 28-35 |
| **Gender** | Female (40%+ of Thai SME owners are women) |
| **Business** | Online fashion/beauty shop |
| **Channels** | Shopee, LINE OA, Facebook Page, Instagram |
| **Revenue** | 50K-200K THB/month |
| **Employees** | 0-2 (often solo + part-time packer) |
| **Location** | Bangkok suburbs or provincial city |
| **Device** | iPhone/Android — manages EVERYTHING from phone |
| **Tech skill** | Uses LINE, Facebook, Shopee daily. Cannot code. Moderate Excel. |
| **English** | Basic — cannot navigate English software comfortably |
| **Monthly tech spend** | 0-1,500 THB (mostly free tools) |

### A Day in Ploy's Life

```
07:00  Wake up, check overnight LINE messages (20-30 unread)
07:30  Reply to messages while making breakfast
08:00  Pack yesterday's orders (10-25 packages)
09:00  Drop packages at Kerry/Flash Express
10:00  Check Shopee/Lazada for new orders, update stock
11:00  Reply to more messages (price? มีสีอื่นไหม? ส่งได้เมื่อไหร่?)
12:00  Quick lunch while scrolling TikTok for content ideas
13:00  Take product photos, write descriptions
14:00  Post on Facebook/Instagram, maybe do a LINE broadcast
15:00  Reply to afternoon message surge
16:00  Process returns/refunds, deal with problems
17:00  Update inventory spreadsheet
18:00  Dinner break
19:00  Reply to evening messages (peak starts)
21:00  PEAK HOUR — customers browsing before bed, messages flood in
23:00  Still replying... "ยังมีไหมคะ?" "ส่งพรุ่งนี้ได้ไหม?"
00:00  Finally sleep. Tomorrow: repeat.
```

### Pain Points (Ranked by Intensity)

#### 1. Message Overload (CRITICAL)
> "ขายของออนไลน์เดี๋ยวนี้ เหนื่อยกว่าเมื่อก่อน 10เท่า (ไม่ได้โม้)"
> *"Selling online nowadays is 10x more exhausting than before (not exaggerating)"*

- Receives **50-200 messages/day** across LINE, Facebook, Shopee, Lazada
- **Over 50% are the same questions**: "ราคาเท่าไหร่?" (price?), "มีสีอื่นไหม?" (other colors?), "ส่งกี่วัน?" (how many days shipping?), "มีของไหม?" (in stock?)
- Loses sales when she can't reply fast enough — customers move on
- **9 PM is peak**: customers browse before bed, but she's exhausted
- Hiring a part-time evening admin: **6,500 THB/month** (her profit margin can't support it)
- **81% of Thai adults message a business at least once/week**

#### 2. Multi-Channel Chaos (HIGH)
- Must manually sync stock across Shopee + Lazada + LINE Shopping
- Average multi-channel seller loses **$2,000+/month** to inventory discrepancies
- Overselling → angry customers → bad reviews → account penalties
- Each platform has different rules, fees, and dashboards
- No unified view of all orders

#### 3. Content Fatigue (HIGH)
> "คิดคอนเทนต์ไม่เป็น ไม่ค่อยมีความคิดสร้างสรรค์"
> *"Can't think of content ideas, lack creative thinking"*

- Must post daily on Facebook, Instagram, maybe TikTok
- Ad creative fatigue: what works one week is invisible the next
- Facebook ad costs up **22%** in 2024
- Hiring a social media manager: **40K-60K THB/month** (impossible)
- She shoots product photos on her phone, writes captions herself

#### 4. Accounting Nightmare (MEDIUM)
- Tracks income/expenses in a notebook or spreadsheet
- VAT compliance is confusing (7% rate, quarterly filing)
- PromptPay transfers mixed with personal account
- End of year = panic
- FlowAccount exists but she hasn't gotten around to setting it up

#### 5. Platform Fee Squeeze (MEDIUM)
- Shopee take-rate jumped to **13-14.5%** (from 4-7% in 2023)
- After fees + shipping + returns, margins are razor thin
- Wants to drive customers to LINE OA (0% fee) but can't manage the chat volume

### What Would Make Ploy Pay

| Solution | Willingness to Pay | Why |
|----------|-------------------|-----|
| AI that auto-replies to 70% of LINE/FB messages | **499-990 THB/mo** | Replaces 6,500 THB/mo admin hire. Saves 3+ hours/day. |
| AI that generates product descriptions & posts | **299-499 THB/mo** | Saves 1-2 hours/day of content creation |
| Multi-channel stock sync | **990-1,990 THB/mo** | Prevents $2,000+/mo in inventory losses |
| All-in-one | **1,990 THB/mo** | If it actually works and is in Thai |

### How to Reach Ploy

- **Facebook Groups**: ขายของออนไลน์, แม่ค้าออนไลน์, ร้านค้าออนไลน์ยุคใหม่ (100K+ members each)
- **TikTok**: Business tips content creators
- **LINE OA**: She already follows business tool accounts
- **Word of mouth**: If her friend uses it and it works, she'll try it
- **Free trial is mandatory**: She won't pay before seeing results

### Profile

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Name** | Ton (ต้น) |
| **Age** | 35-45 |
| **Gender** | Male |
| **Business** | Small restaurant (Thai food, 20-40 seats) |
| **Channels** | GrabFood, LINE MAN, Facebook Page, Google Maps |
| **Revenue** | 100K-500K THB/month |
| **Employees** | 3-8 (kitchen + front of house) |
| **Location** | Bangkok or major city |

| **Tech skill** | Uses LINE, Facebook. Manages GrabFood tablet. Basic computer. |
| **English** | Very basic |
| **Monthly tech spend** | 0-500 THB |

### Pain Points

#### 1. Delivery Platform Commission (CRITICAL)
> "แทบไม่เหลือกำไร"
> *"Barely have any profit left"*

- GrabFood commission: **15-30%** per order
- LINE MAN: similar rates
- On a 200 THB order, he keeps only 140-170 THB before food cost
- Can't quit platforms — 54% of consumers use GrabFood, 49% use LINE MAN
- Wants direct orders via LINE OA but can't manage the chat + ordering flow

#### 2. Multi-Platform Order Management (HIGH)
- Manages GrabFood, LINE MAN, Robinhood, ShopeeFood simultaneously
- Each platform has its own tablet/device at the counter
- No unified dashboard — missed orders during rush hours
- Menu updates must be done separately on each platform
- No consolidated sales analytics

#### 3. Customer Messaging After Hours (HIGH)
- Customers LINE him at 11 PM to ask about tomorrow's specials
- Google Maps questions go unanswered → bad impression
- Facebook reviews need responses but he's too busy cooking
- Weekend/holiday inquiries pile up

#### 4. Staff Management (MEDIUM)
- High turnover — kitchen staff leave frequently
- Scheduling across shifts is manual (whiteboard/LINE group)
- Training new hires is ad-hoc
- Commission/tip tracking is a headache

### What Would Make P' Ton Pay

| Solution | Willingness to Pay |
|----------|-------------------|
| AI that takes LINE OA orders 24/7 (bypass GrabFood commission) | **990-1,990 THB/mo** (saves 15-30% on every order) |
| Unified order dashboard across all platforms | **1,990 THB/mo** |
| AI that replies to Google/Facebook reviews | **499 THB/mo** |
| Staff scheduling + payroll agent | **499-990 THB/mo** |

### How to Reach P' Ton

- **Facebook Groups**: ร้านอาหาร, เจ้าของร้านอาหาร, ร้านอาหารขายดี
- **Word of mouth**: Restaurant owner community is tight-knit
- **GrabFood seller community**: Facebook groups for delivery sellers
- **On-site sales**: Visit food districts, night markets, restaurant zones

- Walk-ins sometimes get served before appointment holders

- No systematic way to remind customers to rebook
- Birthdays, anniversaries = missed upsell opportunities
- Before/after photos — has them but no organized system
- Loyalty program is a paper stamp card or nothing

#### 4. Bilingual Needs (for tourist areas) (MEDIUM)
- Foreign customers message in English
- Thai staff can't always respond well in English
- Google Maps reviews in English need replies
- Menu/price list needs bilingual versions

## First 100 Customers: The Wedge

### Target: Online Sellers (Persona 1) — "Nong Ploy"

**Why start here:**

1. **Largest segment**: 3.26M SMEs, majority are micro sellers
2. **Clearest pain**: message overload is universal and intense
3. **Fastest time-to-value**: connect LINE OA → AI replies in minutes
4. **Easiest to reach**: concentrated in Facebook groups
5. **Viral potential**: sellers talk to each other constantly
6. **Simple product**: chat agent, not complex multi-step workflow
7. **Clear ROI**: "this replied to 200 messages while you slept"

### The First Product: "AI แอดมิน" (AI Admin)

```
What it does:
1. Connect your LINE OA (3 clicks)
2. Upload your product catalog (photos + prices + descriptions)
3. AI auto-replies to customer questions 24/7
4. Hands off to you when it can't answer
5. Dashboard shows: messages handled, time saved, potential sales

What it costs:
- Free: 500 messages/month (enough to see it work)
- Starter: 499 THB/month for 10,000 messages
- Business: 1,990 THB/month for unlimited + analytics

What it replaces:
- Part-time evening admin: 6,500 THB/month
- Your 3+ hours/day of repetitive chat replies
- Lost sales from slow responses during peak hours (9 PM+)
```

1. **Would you use a free AI that auto-replies to your LINE OA messages?**
   - Survey 50 sellers in Facebook groups

   - This builds our template library

   - Tests price sensitivity

## Key Behavioral Insights

### How Thai SMEs Adopt Software

```
                    Awareness → Interest → Trial → Adoption → Expansion
Thai SME flow:      Facebook    Friend      Free     "มันใช้     "ใช้อีก
                    Group       recommends  tier     ได้ว่ะ!"    feature หน่อย"
                    post        it          3 days   (It works!)  (Try more)
```

### Critical Design Principles

1. **Mobile-first** — 69% of Thai web traffic is mobile. Ploy manages her shop from her phone.
2. **Thai-only UI** — English settings pages are instant drop-off points
3. **3-click setup** — Connect LINE OA → upload catalog → done
4. **Show ROI immediately** — "AI ตอบไปแล้ว 47 ข้อความวันนี้" (AI replied to 47 messages today)
5. **Human-in-the-loop** — 79% of Thais require human validation. "AI ตอบ แต่คุณตรวจได้"
6. **PromptPay billing** — Don't require credit card. Bank transfer or QR.
7. **Facebook community** — Support via Facebook Group, not email tickets
8. **Free forever tier** — Thai SMEs use free tools indefinitely. Conversion = demonstrating clear value.

### Thai Consumer AI Trust Segments (SCBX Research, Jan 2026)

| Segment | % | Behavior | Our Strategy |
|---------|---|----------|-------------|
| Smart Minimalist | 36% | Uses AI selectively, verifies results | Show accuracy metrics, human override |
| Skeptical Practitioner | 34% | Open but wary | Free tier, gradual trust-building |
| Curious Enthusiast | 20% | Eager adopter | Early beta users, evangelists |
| AI Resistant | 10% | Avoids AI | Not our target |

**70% of Thai consumers are cautious adopters** — trust and simplicity are non-negotiable.

### The Key Insight

> The competition is not other AI tools. The competition is **"doing it manually"**.
>
> 84% of Thai SMEs are micro businesses. They don't use CRMs, chatbots, or automation.
> They reply to messages by hand, track inventory in notebooks, and create content by gut feel.
>
> The bar is not "better than Zapier." The bar is "better than doing it yourself at midnight."
