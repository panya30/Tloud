# Tloud

**Thai-first AI Agent Builder & Deployment Cloud**

Build AI agents without code. Deploy to Thailand's cloud. Speak Thai natively.

---

## What is Tloud?

Tloud is a no-code platform that lets Thai businesses build, deploy, and manage AI agents — without writing a single line of code. Think "Zapier + Vercel for AI" but designed from the ground up for Thailand.

## Why Thailand? Why Now?

- **3.26M SMEs** with near-zero AI adoption (only 17.8% use AI today)
- **73% of Thai businesses** say they're preparing to adopt AI
- **No Thai-first AI agent platform** exists — only English-only global tools or narrow chatbot-only players
- AWS/Google/Azure just launched Thai cloud regions in 2025 ($8.5B+ investment)
- Government committed **THB 25B ($770M)** to AI development through 2027
- Thai-language LLMs are maturing (Typhoon, OpenThaiGPT)

## The Gap

| What Exists | What's Missing |
|-------------|---------------|
| Botnoi/ZWIZ = chatbots only | General-purpose AI agents |
| Zapier/Make = English only | Thai-first UX |
| n8n/Flowise = needs devs | True no-code for SMEs |
| AWS/GCP = generic cloud | AI-native deployment |

## Core Bet

Thai SME owners manage their entire business from a smartphone via LINE and Facebook. They can't code, don't read English documentation, and won't pay $29/month for tools priced for the US market. But they desperately need AI to handle repetitive chat, inventory, content, and admin tasks that consume their days.

**Tloud meets them where they are**: mobile-first, Thai-native, LINE-integrated, PromptPay billing, starting free.
